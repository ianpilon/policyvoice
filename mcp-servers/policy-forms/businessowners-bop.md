# Businessowners Coverage Form (BOP) — stand-in wording

## Covered Property
Covered Property means Buildings and Business Personal Property as described in the Declarations for which a Limit of Insurance is shown. Buildings include completed additions, fixtures, and permanently installed machinery and equipment. Business Personal Property includes property owned by the insured and used in the business, including stock, furniture, fixtures, and the insured's use interest in tenant improvements and betterments.

## Covered Causes of Loss
Covered Causes of Loss means direct physical loss unless the loss is excluded or limited. We do not pay for loss to the interior of any building or to personal property in the building caused by rain, snow, sleet, ice, sand or dust, whether driven by wind or not, unless the building first sustains damage by a Covered Cause of Loss to its roof or walls through which the rain, snow, sleet, ice, sand or dust enters.

## Business Income and Extra Expense
We will pay for the actual loss of Business Income you sustain due to the necessary suspension of your operations during the period of restoration. The suspension must be caused by direct physical loss to property at the described premises from a Covered Cause of Loss. We also pay necessary Extra Expense you incur during the period of restoration that you would not have incurred if there had been no direct physical loss.

## Exclusions — Water
We will not pay for loss caused directly or indirectly by flood, surface water, waves, tides, tidal waves, overflow of any body of water, or their spray, all whether driven by wind or not; or water that backs up or overflows from a sewer, drain or sump. Such loss is excluded regardless of any other cause or event that contributes concurrently or in any sequence to the loss.

## Deductible
We will not pay for loss in any one occurrence until the amount of loss exceeds the Deductible shown in the Declarations. We will then pay the amount of loss in excess of the Deductible, up to the applicable Limit of Insurance. When a percentage windstorm or hail deductible is shown, it is calculated on the Limit of Insurance for the affected property and applies to loss caused by windstorm or hail.

## Duties In The Event Of Loss
You must see that the following are done in the event of loss to Covered Property: give us prompt notice of the loss, including a description of the property involved; give a description of how, when and where the loss occurred; take all reasonable steps to protect the Covered Property from further damage; and permit us to inspect the property and records proving the loss and to examine your books and records. You must also send us a signed, sworn proof of loss within 60 days after our request.
