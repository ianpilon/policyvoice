# HO-3 Homeowners Policy (base form HO 00 03) — stand-in wording

## Coverage A — Dwelling
We cover the dwelling on the residence premises shown in the Declarations, including structures attached to the dwelling, and materials and supplies located on or next to the residence premises used to construct, alter or repair the dwelling. We do not cover land, including land on which the dwelling is located. Loss to Coverage A property caused by a Peril Insured Against is settled on a replacement cost basis, subject to the loss settlement condition.

## Coverage C — Personal Property
We cover personal property owned or used by an insured while it is anywhere in the world. Our limit of liability for personal property usually located at an insured's residence, other than the residence premises, is 10% of the Coverage C limit, or $1,000, whichever is greater. Special limits of liability apply to certain classes of property, including money, securities, jewelry, watches, furs, and firearms.

## Section I — Perils Insured Against
We insure against direct physical loss to property described in Coverage A and Coverage B unless the loss is excluded or limited. We do not insure for loss to the interior of a building, or property inside a building, caused by rain, snow, sleet, sand or dust, unless a covered peril first damages the building causing an opening through which the rain, snow, sleet, sand or dust enters. We do not insure for loss caused by wear and tear, marring, deterioration, mechanical breakdown, or settling, cracking, shrinking or expansion.

## Section I — Exclusions, Water Damage
We do not insure for loss caused directly or indirectly by water damage, meaning: flood, surface water, waves, tidal water, overflow of a body of water, or spray from any of these, whether or not driven by wind; water or water-borne material which backs up through sewers or drains, or which overflows or is discharged from a sump, sump pump or related equipment; or water below the surface of the ground which exerts pressure on or seeps or leaks through a building, foundation, or other structure. Such loss is excluded regardless of any other cause or event contributing concurrently or in any sequence to the loss.

## Section I — Exclusions, Mold, Fungus or Wet Rot
We do not insure for loss caused by mold, fungus or wet rot. This exclusion does not apply when mold, fungus or wet rot results from water damage that is otherwise covered under Section I of this policy. Coverage related to mold may be added by the Limited Fungi, Wet or Dry Rot, or Bacteria endorsement, which sets a separate aggregate sublimit.

## Coverage D — Loss Of Use
If a loss covered under Section I makes that part of the residence premises where you reside not fit to live in, we cover the Additional Living Expense, meaning any necessary increase in living expenses incurred by you so that your household can maintain its normal standard of living. We also cover Fair Rental Value if the part rented or held for rental is not fit to live in. Payment will be for the shortest time required to repair or replace the damage, or, if you permanently relocate, the shortest time required for your household to settle elsewhere.

## Section I — Conditions, Your Duties After Loss
In case of a loss to covered property, we have no duty to provide coverage under this policy if the failure to comply with the following duties is prejudicial to us. These duties must be performed by you, an insured seeking coverage, or a representative of either: give prompt notice to us or our agent; protect the property from further damage and keep a record of repair expenses; prepare an inventory of damaged personal property showing quantity, description, and amount of loss; and, within 60 days after our request, submit to us a signed, sworn proof of loss.

## Section I — Conditions, Loss Settlement
Covered property losses are settled at replacement cost without deduction for depreciation, subject to the following: if at the time of loss the amount of insurance in this policy on the damaged building is 80% or more of the full replacement cost of the building immediately before the loss, we pay the cost to repair or replace, without deduction for depreciation, but not more than the limit of liability or the necessary amount actually spent to repair or replace.

## Deductible
Unless a separate hurricane or windstorm deductible is shown in the Declarations, the deductible shown in the Declarations applies to each loss. A separate hurricane deductible, when shown, is calculated as a percentage of the Coverage A limit and applies in place of, not in addition to, the all other perils deductible for loss caused by a hurricane.
